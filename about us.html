ABOUT US

1. City Market - Gothatuwa



City Market-Gothatuwa is committed to providing organic and sustainable products to its customers. Our organic produce section features a variety of fruits and vegetables grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. We also offer a selection of health foods and supplements to support a holistic lifestyle. Additionally, customers can find eco-friendly products that promote environmental conservation.



2. City Market - Borella



City Market-Borella is located in the heart of the city. It offers a wide selection of fresh produce sourced directly from local farms. Customers can shop for different varieties of dairy products from our deli counter and cosmetic and skin care products.
